Rules of Play

1) Be honest with points, you will only be cheating yourself.

2) Make sure you select your name, team and date you are submitting for when completing the form. That way we can correctly keep track of your points.

3) Evidence must be submitted for every activity, this can be a photo, a fitness tracker, photo of a pedometer. If you click on the ‘Submit Evidence’ tab, this should send you to a Google Form to do this. Alternatively, you can submit directly to hilltopsnorwich@gmail.com

4) Hilltops starts on the 2nd of August. Deadline for points and evidence submission is 10pm on the 16th August. Points after this time will not be counted.

5) We do not round up points. If you run 2.98 miles, you record 2 miles. Push yourself that bit further.

6) Points are not cumulative if you are under the minimum threshold. For example, if you do 15 minutes of Yoga in the morning and another 15 in the afternoon this does not mean you get 4 points for 30 minutes. However, if you pass the minimum threshold with both sessions you can claim points. e.g. 2x 30 mins of yoga can get you 8 points.

7) If it's not on the list, you don’t get points for it. Our list is fairly exhaustive and we have tried to include a range of activities. If you feel that something you have done should be on there, let us know at the end of the competition and we will consider it for next year.
